Becoming AWARE

Sometimes, when fighting the good fight overseas, risking life and limb for your country simply isn't good enough. Sometimes, you need to spice it up, ARG style. Unforums member NashCarey has been busy doing just that, preparing an ARG for the masses to engulf (sorry, couldn't resist) in the near future. AWARE has already begun some of the pregame activities.

From the Unforums:
My wife and I are putting an ARG together right now. She is amazing at webdesign and we are trying to make and [sic] easier puzzle, more interactive version of an ARG that should last 4-5 months and start in July. Pregame should start in mid-April. Being here in Iraq has given me a lot of time to think up a great story, and I hope everyone will be so kind as to play. The equipment and the person I hired (network guy) has already cost me $4800 to get most of the stuff set up. I am not looking for any return on the investment, I am just doing this for fun and as a big thanks to all the people back home that supported me out here. So be on the look out for AWARE.

People have found some of the trail already, and it looks as though there are already some interactive bits involved in a few diferent websites. AS we become AWARE of more details, we will be sure to pass them on.

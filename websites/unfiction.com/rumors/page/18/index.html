NokiaGame2002

Sunday, October 20th, 2002

DanHon clued us in via the Cloudmakers list that the latest iteration of the Nokia Game, is about to launch for 2002. The game is an interactive adventure scheduled to run from November 11 through November 29, 2002, across 25 countries in Europe and the Middle East. This year's game bears a music theme, and will use the Internet, SMS, chat, interactive voice response (IVR) calls and mini-movies distributed over the web to share information and provide clues and instructions to the players.

Free to play, participants need only have access to the Internet, an e-mail address, and a mobile phone with the capacity to receive short messages in order to participate in the game. The top players from each country will be awarded with a brand-new Nokia 3650 phone with integrated camera, video player and camrecorder.

Jaded Media Hallowe’en Contest Update

Friday, October 11th, 2002

A reliable source tells us that the upcoming competition will be structured in four stages. According to the rules, the contest will officially begin at 12:00 noon (Central) on Sunday, October 27th, and our mole adds that the first and second stages will last two hours each, and the final two rounds will each last an hour. So for those of you planning to play, set aside that block of time because it doesn't look like you'll be able to just jump in at the end. As Calvin would say, "FURTHER BULLETINS AS EVENTS WARRANT!"

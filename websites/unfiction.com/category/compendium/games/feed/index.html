notes

An "Extended Reality" Game based around the universe created in the premium cable TV show "ReGenesis," which centered on a fictional Biotech research and aid organization based in Canada and called the North American Biotechnology Advisory Committee, a joint operation between the US, Canada, and Mexico. The game allowed players to experience more of the world they had begun to explore by watching the weekly television show, and let them gain insights into the story and characters that traditional viewers missed.

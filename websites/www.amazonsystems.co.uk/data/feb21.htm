Feb 21 1999 Statistics

The Classified Results of the Contest.

Unfortunately, the last page of the printout with the actual final placings data disappeared and the organisers wiped the file for the afternoon race! The record of the final result comes courtesy of Graham Ives, Captain of Fleet Elite, who, fortunately, videoed the Monitor screen, in an attempt to make a winning entry for "You've Been Framed". Thank you, Graham.

I pioneered the process of transferring the printout via my scanner, through an OCR utility to MS Word, Cut & Pasted to Excel, then using a fancy Excel macro to dump it out in HTML, the HTML file being extensively hand-carved and processed by Codewright. It would probably have been quicker to type it all in, as it took me most of an evening, but it'll be easier next time!

Gil

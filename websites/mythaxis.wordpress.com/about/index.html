About

This is a blog about my favourite books.  I won’t run out of material.  There are 5000 of them. Most, though not all, of the books featured will be given a favourable review. The criterion for inclusion is that I possess the book concerned, and that it reposes on the groaning shelves of my studio.

Also, this blog is the second home of Mythaxis magazine. Stories published in the formal issues of Mythaxis magazine are reproduced here (>Links>Stories), though two are now missing, because the stories were sold elsewhere. New story submissions are welcome.

Mythaxis magazine had a forum, but it was not heavily used. Those reviews that were on that forum will be transplanted here.

From time to time, there may be a blog entry about some fascinating technology.

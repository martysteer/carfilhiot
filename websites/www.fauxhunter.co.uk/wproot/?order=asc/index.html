Found a phoney web site that pretends to be a football ticket service. It calls itself ticketout.co.uk. Its gone now!!!. I found it because one of my friends had tryed to get a ticket for the Arsenal Villa match on Saturday 16 which of course it is sold out. He did a google for an internet site and came up with this site ticketout.co.uk which said they had hundreds. It looked pretty good too with all the right sort of images etc.
He made a booking for two seats and they took his credit card details, but the tickets havent arrived and he cant find a phone no for them.
There are two thins that make it look phoney. The first is it is a ripoff of an Adobe template. What proper crowd does that? But lets say they were in a hurry. But they even left the artifical text in there?!
